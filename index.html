Intro

With it's warm and welcoming weather, it's soothing tranquiling beaches Antalya offers not only a relaxing experience but also a cultural one from sharing their historical events and artifacts at Manavgat to it's magnificent hotels offering views of the indesribable nature which circulates the city. However Antalya is not to be mistaken as a city which only offers its nature, rather the amount of activities that are to be offered are endless. You can sky dive, snorkel, visit different monuments and if your not necessarily the one for all these energetic activites you ca stay in the confort of your hotel which offers spa treatments. Antalya is a city for all!
